TONG HOP INSIGHT - EDA DU LIEU BAT DONG SAN

1. Tong cong co 14,741 tin rao ban BDS, tap trung tai 2 thanh pho lon: Ho_Chi_Minh (9,772 tin) va Ha_Noi (4,969 tin).

2. Gia BDS trung vi toan thi truong la 6.00 ty VND, nhung phan phoi gia bi lech phai manh (mean 8.71 ty > median), cho thay co mot so it BDS gia rat cao keo gia trung binh len.

3. Loai BDS pho bien nhat la 'Nhà ở' (10,297 tin, 69.9%), trong khi loai co gia trung binh cao nhat la 'Văn phòng, Mặt bằng kinh doanh' (21.78 ty VND).

4. Quan/Huyen co gia/m2 trung binh cao nhat (trong nhom >=30 tin) la 'Hoan_Kiem' (Ha_Noi) voi 433.5 trieu VND/m2.

5. Quan/Huyen co nguon cung (so tin rao ban) lon nhat la 'Thu_Duc' (Ho_Chi_Minh) voi 1360 tin.

6. Tuong quan giua Dien tich va Gia la 0.31 - tuong quan duong vua phai, dien tich lon hon co xu huong gia cao hon nhung khong tuyet doi tuyen tinh.

7. Loai cau hinh phong ngu pho bien nhat la 3 phong ngu (5,312 tin).

8. Mot so cot quan trong bi thieu du lieu kha nhieu: huong nha (direction) thieu 75%, so tang (floors) thieu 49%, dien tich su dung (living_size) thieu 62% - can luu y khi phan tich sau hoac thu thap them.
